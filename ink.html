When the Roses Spoke of Eternal Devotion

In the garden of rustles,
Where roses are enlightened by God,
Their petals spread charmingly,
And there I met her when the world was odd.

Hand in hand with her,
In the amusing breeze,
It felt like heaven,
Giving a heart-warming relief.

Wherever I go,
I carry her memories with me,
As she's the most wondrous princess I've ever imagined,
Whenever I'm tensed or worried, she gifts me glee,
Like a dandelion field — boundless and free.
~ Md. Faiyaz
